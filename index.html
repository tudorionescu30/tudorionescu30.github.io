About Me

AV Research
ML Engineer

I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and B.Eng. in Hearing Technology & Audiology graduate, currently working as a Visiting Student Researcher at KAUST in Saudi Arabia.

My passion lies at the intersection of autonomous vehicles and machine learning. At KAUST, I research LiDAR-based 3D object detection, model degradation strategies for edge deployment, and safety-critical perception systems as part of the YALLA project (Faster Detection for Autonomous Vehicles).

Previously, I worked at Fraunhofer IDMT developing CRNN-based acoustic event detection for automotive applications, and completed my studies at Jade University of Applied Sciences in Oldenburg, Germany.
